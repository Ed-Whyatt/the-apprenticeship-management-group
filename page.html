OVERCOME HEADCOUNT RESTRICTIONS AND OUTSOURCE EMPLOYMENT RESPONSIBILITIES.

Our employment services are designed to support companies to take on apprentices with minimal additional responsibility or where they would otherwise be unable to. As a Flexi-Job Apprenticeship Agency we can also provide addtional flexibility to ensure apprenticeships fit organisational needs perfectly.


Our services are perfect for companies who:

  • Have restrictions placed on headcount.
  • Need additional support to employ an apprentice.
  • Have a simple operating model and wish to outsource employment responsibilities.
  • Require a flexible or tailored apprenticeship programme.

Our Outsourced Employment Services Include:

A completely free recruitment service

All responsibility for the wages, tax, National Insurance of the employee.

All responsibility for administration of holidays, sickness & absence.

Issuing of contracts of employment and full requirements against the agency working regulations

The ability to take a flexible approach to apprenticeship programmes, such as using Front Loaded Apprenticeships, Block Training & Accelerated Apprenticeships

A dedicated account manager to arrange training and ensure the employees progression in line with the companies requirements.

Flexibility around duration of employment should host employer circumstances change.

When employing an apprentice, expert advice along the way and a bespoke Apprenticeship Training Plan, detailing the qualifications, training provider and assessments

We have been operating as an employment agency since 2012. We are an approved Flexi-Job Apprenticeship Agency and are quality assured by the Education and Skills Funding Agency (ESFA).
